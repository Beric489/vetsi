The problem

Veterinary hiring is broken on both sides

Practices pay thousands per hire. Candidates can't browse without risking their job. There's a better way.

💸
Agencies charge a fortune
Recruitment agencies charge 10–15% of first-year salary per placement — paid upfront, non-refundable, whether the vet stays or not.
£12k

👁️
Candidates can't browse safely
Most vets are employed and just curious about what's out there. But registering with an agency means CVs going everywhere — including to their current employer.
🎯
Job boards are noise
Generic adverts, identical listings, no culture insight. Candidates apply blind. Practices sift hundreds of irrelevant applications. Everyone wastes time.

How it works

Built for vets. Built for practices.

Two sides of the same platform, designed to work together.

1
Browse every UK practice on a map
All 5,331 UK practices are pre-loaded. Filter by species, ownership type, salary range and distance from your postcode. See real team sizes, Google ratings and practice culture before you reach out.
2
Stay completely anonymous — until you're ready
Express interest anonymously. The practice sees your specialty and years of experience — nothing else. Upgrade to semi-visible or full profile only when you're comfortable. Your current employer never knows.
3
Message practices directly
Send a message to any claimed practice without expressing interest first. Ask about the role, the culture, the team — no commitment. Your name stays private until you decide otherwise.
4
Find your place in vet — for free
vetsi is completely free for veterinary professionals. No subscription, no placement fee, no catch. Browse, enquire and connect with practices across the UK at zero cost.

Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

🗺️
Interactive map
Every UK vet practice pre-loaded. Filter by species, ownership, hiring status and distance. See real ratings and team sizes before you reach out.
👻
Anonymous browsing
Vets can explore, enquire and message practices without revealing their identity. Three-tier privacy: anonymous, semi-visible, or full profile — candidate's choice, always.
💬
Direct messaging
Two-way messaging between vets and practices. No agency in the middle. No commission on the conversation. Your hire, your relationship.
📋
Hiring pipeline
Built-in candidate management for practices. Track everyone from first enquiry through to offer, with pipeline stages and instant notifications.
🐾
Vet Space community
Anonymous Q&A board for UK vets. Discuss salary, cases, CPD and career moves — all under a persistent anonymous identity. No real name required, ever.
📰
Live vet news
Real-time feeds from Vet Times, BVA, RCVS, VetSurgeon and more — curated in one place so you never miss what matters to the profession.
💷
Salary benchmarking
See real market rate data by specialty, experience level and practice type. Know your worth before you walk into any conversation.
⚡
Career Snapshot
A shareable professional card that lets practices see your background at a glance — shared only when you're ready, at the privacy level you choose.
🔔
Instant notifications
Practices are alerted the moment a vet expresses interest or sends a message. No missed leads, no 48-hour agency delays.
