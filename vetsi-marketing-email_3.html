How vetsi works

1
Vets browse — you don't even know they're looking
Registered vets explore all 5,331 UK practices on an interactive map. They can browse anonymously, with no CV sent and no commitment. Many are employed and just curious — vetsi lets them explore safely.
2
They express interest — and progressively share their profile
When a vet likes what they see, they express interest in your practice. They choose how much to share: starting with specialty and years of experience, then their name and written bio, and ultimately their full contact details including email and phone. The more confident they are in you, the more they reveal — so by the time you have their full profile, you know they're serious.
3
You message them directly — no middleman
Reach out through vetsi's built-in messaging. Have a real conversation about the role, the culture, the team. No agency involved. No commission on the outcome.
4
Hire — and keep every penny of the saving
When you make a hire through vetsi, you pay nothing extra. The £99/month subscription is all it costs. On one hire alone, you've saved enough to cover 10 years of vetsi.
