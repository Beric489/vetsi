Am I being paid fairly?
Use the calculator above to check your salary against the current UK market. If you're below the typical range for your role and region, it may be worth having a conversation with your practice — or exploring what else is out there.
Things that can justify above-market pay: certificate holder, specialist interest area, management responsibility, certificate supervision, no OOH commitment from the practice side.

Things that often suppress salary: working for a charity practice, remote rural location, very small team, part-time contract.
